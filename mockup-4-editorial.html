Section II · The House

A small family shop — that kept getting bigger.

We started The Plumbing Stars in 1998 with a battered Ford, a press in the back, and a promise: tell the truth, charge a fair price, and stand behind the work in writing.

Twenty-six years later, we have more trucks and more techs — but the same family on the phone, and the same crew on the second visit that came on the first. Half of our business is from neighbors recommending neighbors, which is exactly how we want it.

If you've called us before, thank you. If you haven't yet, we look forward to meeting you.

Section III · The Method

Four steps. No surprises.

The way the work actually goes when you call us — top to bottom, in writing.

I.

The phone call

A real human answers, day or night. We text back within five minutes, confirm what's wrong, and schedule a window that respects your day.

II.

The diagnosis

Our technician arrives within the window, inspects the line (usually with an HD camera scope), and shows you the footage on the truck's monitor.

III.

The price, in writing

You see a single flat number — and the work that goes with it — before any tool is unpacked. We do not invoice by the hour. Ever.

IV.

The repair, photographed

Most jobs are finished the same afternoon. We send before/after photos and the warranty paperwork to your inbox before we leave the curb.
